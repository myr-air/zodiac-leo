# Suno Song Production Guardrails

Use for source-only song work in `/Users/xiivth/workspaces/mellow-longplay`.

Read first: `AGENTS.md`, `KNOWLEDGE.md`, `docs/provider-platform-boundary.md`, `mellow-longplay/channel.md`, relevant episode `manifest.json`, and `source/songs.md`.

Allowed: original titles, briefs, sectioned lyrics, arrangement notes, Suno `Styles`, `Exclude Styles`, reject criteria, provenance instructions, and re-review triggers.

Blocked: provider access, Suno operation, browser automation, APIs, downloads, uploads, generated media, account mutation, fake provenance, named-artist/song/voice/channel/brand imitation, and positive rights/platform claims.

Require re-review if provider, model, lyrics, title, style prompt, exclude prompt, controls, vocal policy, voice/upload feature, personalization, disclosure, or upload intent changes.
