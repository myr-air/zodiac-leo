Diagnosis rules:

- Song-source issue: repeated structure, weak hook contrast, intro delay, adjacent similarity, vocal/QA artifact, or lyric slop near the drop.
- Visual issue: overlay competes with subject/subtitles, visual act changes too abruptly, mobile crop fails, or the scene is too static/busy for the listener job.
- Subtitle issue: lines are too dense, mismatched to actual sung words, badly timed, low contrast, or distracting during focus sections.
- Metadata issue: title/thumbnail/description promise attracts the wrong listener job or overstates mood/utility.
