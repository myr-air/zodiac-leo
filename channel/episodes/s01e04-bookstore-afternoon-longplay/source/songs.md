| Field | Gate 1 source-shaped value |
|---|---|
| Listener job | Relaxed afternoon listening for reading, browsing, journaling, slow walk-to-bookshop break, and low-effort daydreaming. |
| Theme thesis | Private thoughts grow safer when routines stay small: paper rustle, receipts, and soft customer moments hold shy hope without forcing declarations. |
| Age/safety lane | High-school / young-adult peers, PG, wholesome, non-sexualized, same-age romance only. |
| Base sonic lane | Cozy chill vocal, soft R&B / easy-listening city-pop blend, warm electric keys, clean guitar, rounded bass, soft brushed drums, airy pads, patient chorus motion, and subtitle-friendly lyric pacing. |
| BPM lane | Overall about 74-92 BPM; opener/closer 74-80, middle arc 80-88, one brighter lift up to 92. |
| Vocal lane | Natural adult/young-adult female vocal, warm and intimate without childlike framing or named-voice imitation. |
| Control baseline | Weirdness about 10-14%; Style Influence about 82% unless a track-specific review approves a change. |
| Reserved variation slot | Track 6 is the only planned **vintage upright piano** color. It must stay secondary and emotionally supportive. No other track may claim an additional special spotlight instrument. |
| Motif budget | Receipt paper, shelf edges, cardboard cartons, paper clips, price tags, quiet checkout click, barcode beeps, dust-lift at top shelves, folded tote bags, soft table lamps, gold crescent-vinyl charm. No school objects, no exams, no classroom tools. |
| Lexical budget | Keep per-song noun/object repetition under control. Limit repeated high-salience words for nouns (`paper`, `quiet`, `light`, `waiting`), pronouns, and payoff terms across adjacent tracks. |
| Base excludes | Named imitation, trap, aggressive rap, EDM drops, orchestra build-ups, novelty/childlike vocal, sexualized framing, real-school branding, school materials, rights/platform-safety claims, public release claims. |
| Review blocker | No title-first chorus repetition, no repeated `No ...`, no `Maybe...`, no `Nothing...`, no `one small/soft note/sign/line` formula, and no track that can be read as named artist/song channel imitation. |
